About me

A brief introduction

I'm Kanav Midha, born and raised in Delhi, where curiosity and academics became second nature at Bal Bharati Public School, GRH Marg. Strong fundamentals there, combined with consistent preparation, helped me clear JEE Mains (AIR 33993) and BITSAT with ranks good enough to earn a seat in one of India's most selective engineering programmes.

Today, I'm pursuing a unique dual-degree: a B.E. in Computer Science from BITS Pilani and a B.Sc. in Software Engineering (Honours) from RMIT University, Melbourne. The intersection of Indian academic rigor and Australian industry-focused learning is shaping how I think about software, systems, and the world they live in.

Right now, I'm building serious depth in Data Structures & Algorithms and exploring the Machine Learning ecosystem. I'm driven by a belief that the best engineers sit at the intersection of first-principles thinking and real-world execution.

Beyond engineering, I unwind on the badminton court and spend my reading time on government policy documents, economics, and geopolitics. These pursuits sharpen the same systems-thinking I bring to code.
